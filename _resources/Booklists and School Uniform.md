<p><u>Online Order</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are encouraged to place orders online via this website: <a href="https://www.pacificbookstores.com/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">www.pacificbookstores.com</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Starting Date (for online orders): 6 November 2025</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Closing Date (for online orders): 13 December 2025</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Payment modes accepted: VISA / MASTERCARD / Debit Cards / PayNow</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can arrange for Self-Collection in school on the stipulated dates
given on the website or opt for Home Delivery at a cost of $14.90.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Note: Online purchase is not available for MOE FAS and School Based FAS
students.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><u>Walk-in purchase</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students may purchase textbooks from the Level 1 Classroom D1-02 during
specified dates (Click <a href="https://cms.isomer.gov.sg/sites/moe-woodlandssec/media/files/mediaDirectory/files/editMediaSettings/dates-for-walk-in-purchase-in-school.pdf" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> to
find the dates for walk-in purchases). Please expect longer queues and
waiting times for walk-in purchases.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Payment modes accepted: Cash, PayNow (QR code) and NTUC Care (Back-to-School).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please bring your own bags â€“ a one-time packaging fee will be applied
to all orders ($0.10 for plastic bag and $0.90 for reusable bag) for customers
without your own bag.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>School Uniform</h4>
<p><u>Online Order</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students may place orders online via the website: <a href="https://www.shanghai-uniforms.sg/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">www.shanghai-uniforms.sg/</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Payment modes accepted: PayNow</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please purchase your uniform online <strong>before 15 December 2025</strong> to
ensure delivery before school reopens.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Delivery fee of $10 is applicable for all orders.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p><u>Walk-in purchase</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students may purchase school uniform from Level 1 Classroom F1-03 during
specified dates (Click <a href="https://cms.isomer.gov.sg/sites/moe-woodlandssec/media/files/mediaDirectory/files/editMediaSettings/dates-for-walk-in-purchase-in-school.pdf" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>&nbsp;to
find the dates for walk-in purchases). Please expect longer queue and waiting
times for walk-in purchases.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Payment modes accepted: Cash and PayNow</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please bring your own bags - a packaging fee of $0.10 per plastic bag
will be applied for customers without your own bag.</p>
</li>
</ul>
